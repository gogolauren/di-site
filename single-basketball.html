Prototype Design – Basketball Game Tickets

Independent Work, in "Interactive Design Method" class

The basketball app was designed to call people to come and attend the NBA game on Oct 31,2016. I designed only few steps, and used prepared souvenirs and coupons to attract users. This app serves as an advertisement of the game, so that it must be short and succinct in content, informing users at their first glance. Additionally, the app is better to have the function to share with friends.













The project started with persona generation. It was not only designed for people who love watching basketball games, but also designed as a special event on Halloween. Therefore, except normal fans, more students and families may come on that day. Student discount and group tickets must be easy to access. Additionally, because of the date, the game may lose a number of regular audience. Discount and souvenirs are used as a compensation.
